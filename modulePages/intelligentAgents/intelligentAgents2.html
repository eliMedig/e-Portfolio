Team Work Assessments

For the project, my team and I acted as software consultants and developed a design proposal for an intelligent agent. Our task was to create a system that could perform specific actions for an organization within a chosen domain: Digital Forensics or Academic Research Online. We chose one of these domains and designed an agent to identify, retrieve, process, and store data.

Tasks

  • Identified Requirements: Listed system requirements, such as necessary libraries.
  • Documented Design Decisions: Explained the rationale behind our design choices.
  • Outlined Development Approach: Described the methodology and techniques we would use.
  • Identified Challenges: Discussed potential issues, such as retrieving specific data.
  • Created Graphical Designs: Produced at least two diagrams (e.g., UML sequence or class diagrams) to visualize the system's design.
  • Provided Justification: Justified our design decisions with academic principles and cited relevant literature.
